Website Requirements Document
Computer Science Society (GCU Lahore)
Issued for: Tech Taakra 2025 Web Development Competition
1. Project Overview
The Computer Science Society (CSS) of Government College University Lahore requires a
fully functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly official website.
The website should serve as the central digital presence of the Society — showcasing its
vision, team, events, updates, and opportunities for students to join and engage.
2. Website Objectives
• To digitally represent the Computer Science Society.
• To showcase the society’s mission, events, and achievements.
• To provide students with updates and registration options for upcoming events.
• To serve as an accessible information hub about the society’s team and
announcements.

5. Design Guidelines(Not rigid)
Participants are encouraged to demonstrate both creativity and clarity in their designs.
Color Scheme:
• Primary Colors: Shades of Blue (#003366) and Gold (#FFD700) to represent
professionalism and prestige.
• Secondary Colors: White, light gray, or subtle gradients for contrast.
Typography:
• Use modern, legible fonts (e.g., Poppins, Lato, or Roboto).
• Maintain a hierarchy — bold headings, medium-weight subheadings, and readable
body text.
Layout Recommendations:
• Clean, grid-based layout with sections divided by clear spacing.
• Header (fixed or scrollable), body content, and footer structure.
• Use cards or tiles for team members and events.
• Include hover effects or micro-animations for interactivity.

7. Suggested Tools & Technologies
Participants may use any stack, but the following are recommended:
• Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
• Frameworks: Bootstrap / React /node js/mongo db/mysql/ Next.js (optional)
• Design: Figma or Canva for layout planning
• Hosting (optional): GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel
8. Creative Freedom & Bonus Points
Participants are encouraged to:
• Add creative sections like an interactive event calendar, gallery sliders, or
testimonials.
• Ensure transitions and animations remain smooth and purposeful (not excessive).
• Incorporate a theme or subtle branding element unique to their version.
• Add a dark/light mode toggle (bonus points).
9. Deliverables
Participants must submit:
1. Source code (ZIP file or GitHub repository link)
2. Live demo link (if hosted)
3. Short README file describing the website’s structure, tools used, and extra features.
